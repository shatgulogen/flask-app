Our Story

This platform is for moms who aspire to become software engineers. From learning resources, tips from technical blog posts, a network of talented mom developers who have the same goal, to the hiring connections, we will be your best resource to help you achieve your dream.


With positive supportive network, inpiring and useful posts, to a connection network full of developer talents, we are confident in our service to help a mom just like you, to kick start your career as software developer.
